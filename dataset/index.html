Dataset

We open-source all data corresponding to the 80-task and 30-task datasets used in our multi-task experiments. They can be downloaded below. The two datasets contain 545M and 345M transitions, respectively.

Download:All80 tasks30 tasks

Overview

We release two multi-task datasets with data from 80 and 30 tasks, respectively. The datasets are summarized below.

Domains Tasks Embodiments Episodes Transitions Size Download

DMControl 		30 11 690k 345M 20GB

These datasets are obtained from the replay buffers of 240 single-task TD-MPC2 agents, and thus contain a wide variety of behaviors ranging from random to expert policies. Multi-task agents trained on the above datasets, as well as checkpoints for each of the single-task agents, are available here.
